4. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember preferences such as language and login details, and collect information on usage patterns.

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze site performance and user interactions to improve usability.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertising and measure campaign effectiveness. We only use these with your consent.

You can manage and disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly if cookies are disabled.

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These include secure servers, encryption, firewalls, and regular monitoring of our systems.
