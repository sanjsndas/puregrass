Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I water my garden?
Watering frequency depends on plant types, soil, and seasonal needs. Generally, a few deep watering sessions per week encourage healthy roots over daily surface watering.
Do you use chemical pesticides in gardening?
We prioritize organic and natural pest management to protect your garden's health while minimizing environmental impact.
Can you install irrigation systems for existing gardens?
Yes, we provide expert evaluation and installation for retrofitting irrigation solutions to any existing garden layout.
What maintenance does an irrigation system need?
Regular checks for leaks, nozzle clogs, and timer functionality help keep irrigation efficient and avoid water waste.
Are your services available for commercial properties?
Absolutely, we cater to both residential and commercial clients, tailoring services to property size and usage needs.
