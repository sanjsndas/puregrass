Welcome to Pure Grass Gardening Services. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our services.

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser that help websites function efficiently and provide valuable information to website owners. They enable us to remember your preferences, analyze website traffic, and enhance your browsing experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are widely used industry-standard technologies that allow websites to recognize your device and store information to enhance your visit. They may contain identifiers, site-specific information, or preferences to help customize your experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the operation of our website and cannot be disabled. They enable functions like page navigation, security features, and user authentication.

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site by collecting anonymous usage data. This information allows us to improve functionality, user experience, and identify issues.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember choices you make, such as language preferences, to provide enhanced and personalized features.

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies track your browsing habits to deliver relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns. We use these cookies only with your consent.

3. How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us:

  • Ensure your preferred settings are saved between visits.
  • Analyze website traffic and understand visitor behavior.
  • Personalize content and advertisements based on your interests.
  • Provide you with relevant offers and promotions.
  • Maintain security and prevent fraudulent activity.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may allow third-party services to place cookies through our website, including:

  • Google Analytics – for website traffic analysis.
  • Advertising partners – to assist in delivering targeted marketing.
  • Social media platforms for sharing content or logging in using social accounts.

These third parties have their own privacy policies covering how they collect and use your information. We encourage you to review those.

5. Your Cookie Choices

You have the right to control cookies on your device. The cookie consent dialog at the bottom of this page enables you to accept or decline non-essential cookies.

You can manage cookie preferences through your web browser settings, allowing you to block or delete cookies as you wish. Note that blocking some cookies may affect the performance and functionality of our website.

6. How to Manage Cookies in Your Browser

Most web browsers provide settings to control cookies including:

  • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Site permissions > Cookies and site data.

Please consult your browser’s documentation for detailed guidance.

7. What Happens If You Decline Cookies?

If you decline non-essential cookies, certain features on our website may not function properly. This may include limited personalization of content or marketing communications.

Your choice will be respected, and you can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and returning to the site.

8. Data Protection and Security

Cookies and tracking data collected through our website are handled in compliance with applicable data protection laws. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data and limit access to authorized personnel.

9. Updates to This Cookie Policy

Pure Grass Gardening Services may update this Cookie Policy as necessary. Updated versions will be posted on this page with the revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
